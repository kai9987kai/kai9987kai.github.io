Built-in research notes

  • Modern gut models increasingly include microfluidic flow, peristalsis-like motion, mucus production, co-cultures and sometimes microbiome components.
  • AOP thinking explains risk as linked events from exposure to molecular and tissue-level outcomes, so this app now explains drivers rather than only showing a score.
  • Barrier health is not a single wall: mucus, epithelial cells, tight junctions, immune signalling and region-specific pH/buffering all matter.
  • PubChem lookup is optional and only assists chemical identity/molecular weight; it does not make the model clinically validated.
