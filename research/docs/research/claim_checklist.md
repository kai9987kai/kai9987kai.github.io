# Claim Checklist (Needs Citation / Verification)

Use this before publishing anything beyond a private draft.

## Receptor / Molecular

- Exact structural findings from the 5-HT2A-LSD crystal/cryo-EM work
- Whether EL2 "lid" language is accurate for 5-HT2A specifically in the cited structure
- What is known vs inferred about residence time and subjective duration
- Relative contribution of receptor trafficking/internalization to LSD offset

## Circuit Models

- Evidence quality for CSTC loop disruption as a primary mechanism
- Specific role of thalamic reticular nucleus in psychedelic sensory gating
- Which findings are compound-specific vs generalized across psychedelics

## Electrophysiology

- Strength of evidence for alpha suppression across compounds and doses
- Evidence for traveling-wave reversal (species, modality, sample sizes)
- How directly those findings support predictive-processing / REBUS claims

## Plasticity / Therapeutics

- Which compounds have strongest evidence for critical-period reopening
- Gül Dölen findings by species / behavior domain / compound
- Time window estimates (days vs weeks) and what is established in humans
- BDNF, dendritic spine changes: preclinical vs clinical evidence

## Clinical Framing

- Separate classical psychedelics, dissociatives, and empathogens when making mechanistic claims
- Therapeutic effect sizes and durability by indication (depression, PTSD, addiction, etc.)
- Role of psychotherapy/integration vs pharmacology alone

## Terminology / Style Checks

- Avoid stating hypotheses as settled fact
- Avoid anthropomorphic language where precision matters
- Keep "hallucination" vs "altered perception" distinctions clear
