Research Notes

What is advanced compared with the original?

Model Predictive Control (MPC): Instead of 1-step Dyna-style Q-updates during imagination, agents now project multi-step random-shooting trajectories ($H$ steps) through their learned world model, evaluating the sequence, and executing only the first action. This mirrors modern MBRL architectures like TD-MPC and DreamerV3.

Pseudo-count Curiosity: Intrinsic reward is now driven by $1/\sqrt{N(s)+1}$ pseudo-counts rather than decaying heuristics. This creates a more mathematically stable exploration bonus that naturally balances novel states without vanishing too fast in stochastic environments.

Attention-based Comms: Multi-agent communication uses spatial attention. Shared observations from distant agents are weighted exponentially less than local ones, mimicking Graph Attention Networks (GAT) in multi-agent RL for efficient decentralized map merging.

Visualized Imagination: The model canvas now visually renders the agent's MPC rollout as a glowing projected trajectory, allowing direct observation of the "imagination" phase before action execution.
