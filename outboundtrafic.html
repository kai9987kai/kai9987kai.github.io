Partner integration brief

Give this to a partner that wants secure conversion attribution. It uses the click_id in the destination URL and, when configured, a server-issued ref_token.

Signals sent honestly

  • Standard analytics parameters utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_content, and optional utm_term.
  • Readable referral context ref, ref_site, ref_page, ref_link, and optional partner_id.
  • Unique click identifier click_id lets a partner connect an approved postback or conversion event to this outbound click.
  • Normal browser referrer Browser-controlled Referer information may be sent subject to this page’s referrer policy and user privacy settings.
  • Server-signed referral token Available only when you configure a secure signing endpoint. No signing secret is ever embedded in this HTML file.
  • First-party local diagnostics Optional local storage only. No attempt is made to set, copy, or read another website’s cookies.
