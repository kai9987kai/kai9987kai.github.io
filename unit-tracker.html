Info, health notes & keyboard shortcuts

UK guidance: adults are advised not to regularly drink more than 14 units per week, spread over 3 or more days, with several drink-free days. There is no completely safe level of drinking. If you are concerned about alcohol use, withdrawal, pregnancy, medication interactions, or dependency, seek professional medical help.
