🥭 Mango Chicken

A brighter, smoother, restaurant-style curry card with interactive steps, a cleaner layout, and a print-friendly finish.

Serves 4 people
Prep 10 min
Cook 15 min

🛒 Ingredients

Prep everything first for a faster, smoother cook.

*Make your favourite base curry sauce ahead, or use a good store-bought version to save time.
Creamy curry finish Sweet mango balance One-pan cooking

👩‍🍳 Method

Tap each step to reveal a more detailed cooking cue.

Stir for about 30 seconds until fragrant, then pour in 1 cup of base curry sauce and let it bubble gently.
Only stir if it starts to stick. Add the coconut flour and mango chutney, then pour in the extra ½ cup of base sauce and mix well.
Cook for around 1 minute in the bubbling sauce so the chicken just begins to heat through.
Add the diced mango, then simmer for 4–5 minutes. Add a splash of water if you want a looser sauce.
Taste and adjust salt and pepper, then stir through the garam masala and dried fenugreek leaves. Finish with fresh cilantro.
