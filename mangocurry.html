🥭 Mango Chicken

Serves 4 – Prep: 10 min + base sauce • Cook: 15 min

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp rapeseed or vegetable oil (≈1 oz / 30 ml)
  • 2 Tbsp garlic & ginger paste (≈1 oz / 30 g) 🧄🌱
  • 1 tsp chilli powder (or to taste) 🌶️
  • 1 cup (250 ml) base curry sauce* 🍛
  • 2 Tbsp coconut flour (≈14 g) 🥥
  • 2 Tbsp mango chutney (≈30 g) 🥭
  • ½ cup (125 ml) extra base curry sauce
  • 1 lb (450 g) chicken, bite-sized pieces 🍗
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled & diced 🥭
  • Salt & pepper, to taste 🧂
  • 1 tsp garam masala 🌿
  • 1 tsp dried fenugreek leaves (methi) 🍃
  • Handful fresh cilantro (coriander) leaves, for garnish 🌿
*Make your favourite base curry sauce ahead or grab 1 cup (250 ml) store-bought.

👩‍🍳 Method

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. When it shimmers, add garlic & ginger paste and fry 30 sec. Show ✔️

    Stir in chilli powder until aromatic, then pour in 1 cup (250 ml) base curry sauce. Let it bubble.

  2. Bring to a gentle rolling simmer (only stir if sticking). Show ✔️

    Add coconut flour & mango chutney, then ½ cup (125 ml) more base sauce. Stir well.

  3. Stir in chicken pieces. Show ✔️

    Cook 1 min in bubbling sauce so chicken just begins to heat through.

  4. Fold in diced mango. Show ✔️

    Pour in remaining base sauce. Simmer 4 – 5 min until sauce thickens to your liking. Add splash of water if too thick.

  5. Season & finish. Show ✔️

    Adjust salt & pepper. Sprinkle garam masala & dried methi leaves. Stir to combine.

  6. Garnish & serve. 🌿
