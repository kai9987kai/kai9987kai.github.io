What changed when I press the switch?
Closing the button completes the circuit. Current creates a magnetic field in the coil, pulling an iron armature so a striker hits a chime.
How to fault-find like a real technician
Start at the source, then measure across each section. Full voltage across an open switch or broken wire means the current cannot flow. Very high current with tiny load voltage means a short.
