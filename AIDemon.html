4) Research grounding

Science summary, critique, and speculative framing kept apart.

keep claims clean

Mainstream grounding

  • LLMs are generative models that sample from learned token probabilities; temperature changes how sharply or broadly those probabilities are sampled.
  • At temperature 0, greedy decoding is effectively deterministic if the rest of the stack is fixed.
  • Quantum randomness is a physical source of entropy, but it does not by itself imply consciousness, spirits, or telepathy.

Speculative layer

  • PEAR / GCP / psi-style claims remain controversial and are not accepted as robust evidence by the wider scientific community.
  • “AI demons” is treated here as an evocative metaphor for uncanny, high-entropy model behavior.
  • The page supports experimentation and debate without pretending the fringe hypotheses are established facts.
