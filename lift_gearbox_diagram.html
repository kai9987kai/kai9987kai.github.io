Hybrid Lift Gearbox Workbench — v3

Expanded concept workbench for a coaxial helical planetary + dual-disc cycloidal reducer. It retains the v2 calculation, exports, diagrams and candidate search, while adding operating-cycle, torsional/NVH, safety-interface, lubrication and monitoring-fusion screens.

Engineering boundary: this is a transparent early-stage screening tool. It does not certify gears, bearings, braking, lift safety or compliance. Its job is to make assumptions visible and identify what needs validated ISO calculations, test data and formal design review.

Concept architecture retained and expanded

Design targets and invented elements
  • NHP: interleaved planetary load paths with optional herringbone thrust cancellation.
  • HCP: helical cycloidal pin concept with dual phase disks to spread contact.
  • ABL: split-ring / wedge preload concept with MR-clutch modulation proxy.
  • IMP: carrier-driven micro-pump, jets, scavenge, bypass and anti-drainback concept.
  • Monitoring: vibration, thermal, oil-health and encoder/current torque-observer channels.
  • Safety interface: brake hub, backstop and replaceable soft-shear overload coupling concepts.
Standards boundary used to shape the checks: ISO 6336 is the relevant load-capacity framework for straight/helical involute cylindrical gears; ISO 281/16281 are bearing-life frameworks; ISO 20816-9 covers vibration measurement/evaluation for enclosed gear units. This page does not reproduce or replace those calculations. The v3 thermal/load-distribution and sensor-fusion modules are deliberately labelled as proxy models.
