Before streaming to a real printer

This tool is an experimental slicer and controller. It can send heater, homing, movement, and emergency-stop commands.

  • Keep the printer supervised.
  • Verify nozzle/bed temperatures, model placement, and Z height.
  • Preview the generated G-code path before printing.
  • Do not use for valuable, safety-critical, or unattended prints.
