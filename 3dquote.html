FAQ

Extra information added to reduce back-and-forth before someone submits a quote.

What files can I ask for?

Common formats include OBJ, FBX, GLB/GLTF, STL, PNG textures, blend files, and rendered images.

Can it be game-ready?

Yes. Mention the game engine, polygon target, texture size, and whether you need optimisation.

Can I send references?

Yes. Paste reference links in the form so the style, scale, and details are easier to understand.
