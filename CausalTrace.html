What v0.7 changes

v0.6 diagnosis: the direction/cadence mean was positive but only 4/10 seeds improved. That misses the replication rule even though its mean confidence interval barely cleared zero.
Correction: discovery evidence and verification evidence are now separate. The guard cannot deploy a model correction until its frozen posterior predicts a later probe packet accurately.
Minimal override: a verified model may only replace the base action for an immediate predicted goal, a certified hazard avoidance, or a materially shorter safe transition.
1 · DiscoveryFixed unscored probes create a posterior over hidden gravity direction and echo cadence.
2 · FalsificationLater probes test the frozen posterior. Verification does not update it.
3 · DeploymentVerifiedGuard acts only when accuracy, confidence, and a local safety certificate all clear their thresholds.
