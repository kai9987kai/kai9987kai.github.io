What V5 fixes and adds

V5 keeps marker AR as the reliable baseline, fixes several edge-case bugs, and adds a safer creative studio layer: diagnostics, pause/reset/calibration, shockwave bursts, flickering light, better capture fallbacks, visibility-aware pausing, improved preset migration, and a cleaner WebXR migration path.

Novel upgrade ideas built into the architecture

  • Sensor-reactive fire: optional tilt wind maps phone orientation into flame drift.
  • Thermal atmosphere: animated heat-haze plane and pulsing ground rings sell the illusion without heavy models.
  • Performance governor: particle budget rises or falls based on measured FPS and marker stability.
  • Creative capture: snapshot, short WebM recording when supported, exportable presets, and shareable JSON.
  • WebXR-ready: detects immersive AR support so a future markerless hit-test scene can be added cleanly.
