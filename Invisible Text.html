Safety and testing notes

Zero-width text can be useful for learning, watermarking, and controlled lab testing, but it can also confuse readers and tools. The sanitizer removes hidden Unicode controls from copied text. The reveal view highlights hidden characters so you can audit suspicious text before sharing it.

This tool does not send text anywhere. Use it only on content you own or have permission to test.
