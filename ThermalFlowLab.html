How it works

Temperature and velocity live on an Eulerian grid. Each step adds source terms, advects fields with a corrected semi-Lagrangian pass, removes divergence with a pressure solve, and restores small vortices. Right-click removes objects. This is a qualitative 2D model, so do not use it for ventilation safety or design decisions.
